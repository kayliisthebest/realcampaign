Statistics from 24 Texting and Driving Statistics 2019 Update written by Nikola Djurkovic

Driving while texting causes 1.6 million car accidents in the US every year. 21% of US drivers reported sending emails or text messages while on the road. 14% of all fatal crashes in the US involved the use of a cell phone. In 2018, the number of deaths related to cell phone use in car accidents was 4,637 in the US alone. Almost 390,000 injuries occur annually in the US due to texting while driving.
